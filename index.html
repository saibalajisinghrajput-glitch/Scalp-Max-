Is this shampoo suitable for dry scalp?

Yes! This shampoo is specially formulated to treat both dandruff and dry scalp. It contains moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil and aloe vera that hydrate your scalp while fighting dandruff.

For best results, use this shampoo 2-3 times per week. For severe dandruff, you can use it daily for the first week, then reduce to 2-3 times per week for maintenance.

Yes! Our formula is gentle and safe for colored and chemically treated hair. It won't strip your hair color while effectively treating dandruff.
